Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from enterprise custody?
Enterprise custody means trusting third parties with your Bitcoin and paying ongoing fees. Third parties can freeze, seize, or lose your assets. Our approach eliminates counterparty risk entirely - you own and control everything with no custody fees or dependencies on external parties.
Is this secure enough for significant Bitcoin holdings?
Professional implementation delivers enterprise-grade security without counterparty risk. Multi-signature configurations, hardware security modules, geographic distribution, and comprehensive operational procedures provide institutional-level protection with complete ownership.
What if something goes wrong - do we have support?
Yes, but support maintains your independence and eliminates counterparty risk. We provide guidance and expertise without accessing your systems. Your team retains complete control while getting expert assistance when needed.
How long does implementation take and what's included?
Timeline depends on complexity - typically 2-6 weeks for complete setup. Includes all hardware procurement, professional configuration, security testing, team training, and comprehensive documentation. You get everything needed for independent operations.
What happens if our team changes?
Complete documentation and training materials enable smooth transitions. New team members can learn the system through comprehensive guides, procedures, and ongoing support options. Your independence isn't dependent on specific individuals.
What if I don't see value from our first session?
If you don't find clear, actionable value in our initial consultation, don't pay. We're confident in the insights and improvements we can identify in most Bitcoin treasury setups.
